# Discord — Target-Market Copy

Paste-ready posts for Discord communities. Each block is copy-paste ready.

**Read this first.** Discord communities are relationship-first and allergic to drive-by promotion. Before posting:
- **Check the server rules.** Many have a dedicated `#self-promo` / `#showcase` channel and ban links elsewhere. Some require a role or minimum activity.
- **Be a member first.** Contribute in conversation before you post about CrewPort.
- **Talk like a person, not a billboard.** Lead with what you built or what's useful; let people ask for the link.

Good fits: AI builder / agent-dev servers, indie hacker and maker servers, LLM/ML communities (operators); startup, founder, and SaaS-buyer servers (clients).
